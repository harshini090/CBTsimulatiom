Scenario Introduction

You are stepping into the role of a cognitive-behavioral therapist. Your patient, Sarah, is a 28-year-old marketing professional who has been experiencing significant distress related to her work performance.

In recent weeks, she has been reporting high levels of anxiety, self-doubt, and fear of failure, particularly after receiving feedback from her supervisor.

Learning Objectives

  • Identify automatic negative thoughts and cognitive distortions.
  • Apply Socratic questioning to explore the patient's rigid belief systems.
  • Guide the patient toward cognitive restructuring and evidence examination.
  • Assign appropriate behavioral homework (e.g., thought record).

End of Scenario Assessment
